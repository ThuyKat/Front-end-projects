Top three activities to do in Hanoi

Go shopping in Hanoi Old Quarter

Dong Xuan and Night Markets are a great places to spend your day out shopping.

Visit beautiful temples with hundred years of history

Temple of Literature (Văn Miếu) reflects the city's deep spiritual heritage and cultural significance.

Experience the city's rich culinary heritage.

Enjoy vibrant street food such as pho (noodle soup), bun cha (grilled pork with noodles), and egg coffee
